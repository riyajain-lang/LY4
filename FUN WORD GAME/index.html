🧸 Fun Word Game

Pick the word that means the same!

1. The sun is very big.

A. Large
B. Tiny
C. Cold
D. Fast

2. She feels happy.

A. Sad
B. Glad
C. Angry
D. Sleepy

3. The kitten is very small.

A. Big
B. Little
C. Loud
D. Fast

4. He can run fast.

A. Slow
B. Quick
C. Tall
D. Heavy

5. The baby is sleepy.

A. Tired
B. Hungry
C. Happy
D. Loud
⭐ Score: 0 / 5

🔒 Answers & Simple Meanings

1. Large — "Big" means large.
Closest distractor: Tiny (very small).

2. Glad — "Happy" means glad.
Closest distractor: Sad (opposite).

3. Little — "Small" means little.
Closest distractor: Big (opposite).

4. Quick — "Fast" means quick.
Closest distractor: Slow (opposite).

5. Tired — "Sleepy" means tired.
Closest distractor: Hungry (different feeling).
